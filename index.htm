Welcome to BITS Hyderabad!


So what is SWD?

SWD stands for Student Welfare Division. As the name suggests, it works for the students, by the students, inducting new members to the SWD family every year. SWD nucleus manages everything from mess registration to wing allotment. It's the commitee which ensures that all the students have a smooth campus experience.


How to reach SWD?

The best way to do so is to mail the SWD at swd@hyderabad.bits-pilani.ac.in or swdnucleus@hyderabad.bits-pilani.ac.in.
